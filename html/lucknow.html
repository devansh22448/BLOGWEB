Lucknow – Where Royalty Meets Tradition

Things to Explore in Lucknow

  • Bara Imambara – Iconic 18th-century monument famous for its central hall and Bhool Bhulaiya (labyrinth).
  • Chota Imambara – Stunning monument known for its intricate chandeliers and Islamic calligraphy.
  • Rumi Darwaza – Majestic 60-feet tall gateway, also called the “Turkish Gate.”
  • Hazratganj Market – Bustling hub for shopping, food, and Lucknow’s Nawabi charm.
  • Lucknow Residency – Historical site linked to the First War of Independence in 1857.
  • Ambedkar Memorial Park – Grand park with unique architecture and stone carvings.
  • Tunday Kababi – World-famous kebabs and Awadhi cuisine you can’t miss.
