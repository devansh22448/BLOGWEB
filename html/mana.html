Mana — India’s Last Village

Things to Explore in Mana Village

  • Vyas Gufa – Cave where Ved Vyas is believed to have composed the Mahabharata.
  • Ganesh Gufa – Said to be where Lord Ganesha wrote down the epic.
  • Saraswati River – See its roaring flow before meeting the Alaknanda.
  • Bheem Pul – Natural rock bridge linked to the Pandava legend.
  • “Last Tea Shop of India” – Iconic stop for chai with a story.
  • Swargarohini Path – Mythic route of the Pandavas’ ascent.
  • Easy Trekking Trails – Short hikes with stunning Himalayan vistas.
