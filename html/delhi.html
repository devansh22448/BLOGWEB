🍴 Exploring Delhi Street Food

Delhi, the heart of India, is famous for its rich culture and mouth-watering street food. From Chandni Chowk’s parathas to spicy chaat at Connaught Place, my trip was filled with flavors and cultural experiences. The city is truly a blend of history, tradition, and modern vibes.

Highlights of Delhi

  • 🍲 Chole Bhature at Chandni Chowk
  • 🍢 Kebabs at Jama Masjid lanes
  • 🏰 Visiting Red Fort and India Gate
  • 🛍️ Shopping at Sarojini Nagar
